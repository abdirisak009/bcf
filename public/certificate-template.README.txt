Place your Acrobat PDF here as:

  Certificate-tem.pdf

The Go backend does not use AcroForm fill for the final PDF. It removes form field
widgets (so grey field boxes are not shown), then draws names, dates, and IDs on
top with Helvetica (large bold student name and course title).

You may keep AcroForm fields in the template for authoring reference; typical names:

  studentName, trainingName, fromDate, toDate, certificateNo, date, sigin

Overlay positions are tuned in backend/internal/pdf/certificate_pdf.go (certOverlay*
constants). If text does not line up with your design, adjust those values.

Backend .env example (local Next.js on port 3000):

  CERTIFICATE_TEMPLATE_URL=http://127.0.0.1:3000/Certificate-tem.pdf

Restart the Go API after changing .env.
